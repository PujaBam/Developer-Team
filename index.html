    Our team would only validate the idea of skilled founders with excellent business plan, ideas and solutions.

    2. Build a great team

    Founders can't grow the business alone. We help startups to build a great team to run a successful business.

    3. Develop a great product

    Excellent businesses idea results in the great product that can survive on the competitive market in a sustainable way.

    4. Fundraise

    We provide pre-seed funding in the earliest stage and also help startups to gain investors in the future.

We even encourage to apply individual without a startup idea but with technical skills. The received applications are accessed on a case-by-case basis by Ombryo team. To be honest, the chances of acceptance are minimal but if you feel you’ve got what it takes-please consider applying.

    Submit Application

    2

    Case Assessment

    Each of application will be carefully analyzed by our team of experts around the globe to study the feasibility and complexity.

    3

    One on one meeting

    The selected applicants will be invited to one-to-one meetings to discuss their vision and requirements in detail.

    4

    Selection

    The applicants selected after one-to-one meetings will work with our team to fulfill all their requirements
